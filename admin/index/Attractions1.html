Local Attractions





MOUNTAIN COASTER

 Get ready for a unique coaster experience in the Poconos. Our signature 4500' steel-track mountain coaster in the Poconos zooms through the trees and down the steep slopes of Camelback Mountain. Climb aboard and experience PA's only mountain coaster! Save time by adding parking when purchasing tickets.


4000’ ZIP FLYER/RIDER

 Zip down 4000' of zipline with your feet dangling over 180 feet in the air. Experience the thrill of flying while taking in the beautiful Pocono Mountain Scenery on the longest zipline in PA.




SWITCHBACK UTV TOUR

 Explore the road less traveled in the driver's seat of a UTV. Our Ultimate Terrain Vehicles have enough power, traction, and fun to navigate the picturesque Pocono mountain-scape and trail area.
