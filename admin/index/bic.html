THINGS ARE HEATING UP AT BeRRELLI’S ITALIAN CHOPHOUSE.

After a long day of adventuring with the family, come “Wine” down at your new favorite Pocono’s Restaurant. Our robust menu ranges from Prime Dry-Aged Chops to Classic Italian Pastas, with the new staple of your dine-out diet waiting to be discovered. Various worldly wines and delicious cocktails will keep you warm throughout the evening or maybe that’s just the feeling of the whole family laughing together again. In addition to our indoor seating, we have added an outdoor deck for those who enjoy more of that fresh mountain air.
